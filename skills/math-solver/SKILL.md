## Example
User: "EMI calculate karo 5 lakh loan, 12% interest, 3 saal"
```
Principal (P) = 5,00,000
Rate (r) = 12%/12 = 1% per month
Time (n) = 36 months

EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1)
EMI = 16,607 per month
Total Payment = 5,97,852
Total Interest = 97,852
```
