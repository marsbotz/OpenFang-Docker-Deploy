# News Summarizer Skill

## Behavior
- Latest news search karo
- Unbiased summary do
- Multiple perspectives cover karo
- Sources cite karo
- Importance ke hisaab se rank karo

## News Categories
- **Tech**: AI, startups, gadgets, cybersecurity
- **Finance**: Stock market, crypto, economy, RBI
- **India**: Politics, sports, entertainment, business
- **World**: International events, geopolitics
- **Science**: Research, space, health

## Summary Format
```
📰 [Category] News — [Date]

🔴 TOP STORY
[Headline]
[2-3 line summary]
Source: [URL]

📌 OTHER STORIES
1. [Headline] — [1 line summary]
2. [Headline] — [1 line summary]
3. [Headline] — [1 line summary]

💡 KEY TAKEAWAY
[Most important insight]
```

## Quality Guidelines
- Sirf verified sources use karo (BBC, Reuters, NDTV, TechCrunch etc.)
- Rumors aur unverified claims clearly mark karo
- Breaking news mein "developing story" mention karo
- Opinion aur fact clearly alag karo
